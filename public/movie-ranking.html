The Best Movies of All Time

Adam's Movie Recommendations

1. The Lord of The Rings: The Two Towers

This is the second film in a trilogy, but there are powerful moments, incredible scenery, and intense action that make this worth a watch.

2. Fantastic Mr. Fox

Beautiful colors, charming animation, and based on a children's novel by the brilliant Roald Dahl.

3. The Princess Bride

A classic adventure film. Daring fights, true love, solid comedy. What's not to like?
